About US

Being from an engineering background I found out an universal law of creativity that

“Creativity can neither be created nor can be destroyed,
It is simply transformed from generations to generation”

That is the reason, we at Playhouse Media Works follow a rich tradition of offering practical & value-based service that is upheld since its founding.

Since its inception in the year 2010, PMW has grown as an entity with a high value of commitment to provide an excellence, with abundance of creativity and with an extremely devoted creative team. We understand the importance of media engagement with business and society; we behave in a socially responsible manner and encourage our clients to do the same. We want our clients to have a satisfied, happy and enriching experience with us and to showcase themselves to the universe with the motivation and confidence to become responsible leaders who can make a significant contribution to the wealth and well-being of the societies in which they work.

Never thought I would ever become a film-maker but I remember an instance when I was in 8th grade where I told my friend that I wanted to become a film-maker, maybe that moment just worked for me and after 14 years I got to direct my first feature film. Many of my friends and the people from my huge circle ask me that how did I transitioned from civil engineer to a film-maker to which I pass a pleasant smile and I narrate the story, which is my favorite hobby since childhood. I consider my timeline to be a reverse engineered one because I became an editor, then I became a director and surprisingly I became a scriptwriter later only because I always believed “never stop listening to your mind and soul”.

With an intention to serve our clients with unique media solutions, I am confident we can offer them an exceptional experience
