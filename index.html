Our Services

Creating a Plan

We understand your key aim for the video and its core base. On the basis of our discussion, we plan out various stages of video production, taking your budget and target market into account.

Scripting

A video script basically features everything that is going to be seen on it, including its settings, props, voice-overs, animations, actors, and more. We take your feedback subsequent to formulating the script and make any needed changes to it.

Pre-Production

After scriptwriting and story binding, we move on to the pre-production process. In this stage, we set-up all the components needed for the actual filming process, including hiring actors or voice-actors, getting the needed equipment and permits.

Filming

This is the most important stage of the video production, as the actual filming process takes place here. Having a number of expert professionals in our team, we are able to complete this step as swiftly as possible.

Editing

The most crucial and life giving area of the video production. On the basis of our prior discussion, we undergo the editing process. It includes creation of timeline, adding effects, color correction and processing. The animated projects directly take a bridge from pre production to the editing process

Project Delivery

With the completion of the editing process, we handover the final video to you.

Frequently Asked Question?

The answers to the common questions we come across

How much does video and animation production cost?
The most common question we come across with almost all the clients. Well the answer to this depends upon the following factors
- Whether we’re filming in one location or several
- How long we need to allow for filming and how large a crew we need
- How complex the edited video is – especially if it involves animated elements
- Whether we’re producing a single video or several related video
How does our rates compare to others video production companies?
We are not the most expensive, and we certainly are not the cheapest shop in the metro city, however, we are able to maximize any budget and do more with less. Plus, our videos are super fun to make and watch.
Will you help us develop content for our commercial?
Ofcourse! We offer a complete video production service and will assist in storyboarding, scripting, and any pre-production needs you have. Content development is a strong suite of ours, , we are established to create kick-ass video content.
I need video footage without editing..?
Yes. We can shoot video and hand over the unedited digital files to you.
I have footage that needs editing..?
Yes. We can edit your existing video footage. Reach out and we can get you a quote.
Will there be additional cost for changes after approval?
Yes. Changes that are done after the approval of the script Will incur additional expense. We will always discuss those costs with you beforehand as to ensure it is approved to make the change.
How many re-editions do I get after approval of the first cut?
Playhouse has a structured approval process. You have 2 opportunities to make changes on the finished video.
Why are video shoots are as per full days and half-days?
In our industry, the half-day ( 0 to 6 hours) and full day (0-12 hours) rates are standard. On any shoot, our crews put in a considerable amount of time developing a shot list, checking off shoot logistics, preparing equipment, transporting gear and setting up. Every shoot is a 4-hour commitment, minimum.
